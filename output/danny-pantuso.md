Here's what's interesting to me as an investor: the startups that couldn't be replicated stand out in a completely different way now. Defensibility used to mean you got there first. Now it has to mean something the agent can't just reconstruct from your landing page and docs.

This isn't a hit piece on YC. YC produces incredible founders. But the question it raises is real: if the product layer is increasingly commoditized by agents, where does the actual value sit?

TBD on whether most of the ecosystem is ready to have that conversation.
